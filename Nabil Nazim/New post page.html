What Is A Forum?

A forum is an online platform where individuals gather to discuss and exchange ideas on various topics. It serves as a virtual gathering place where members of particular communities or interest groups can have discussions, ask questions, impart knowledge, and express opinions. Forums offer a beneficial setting for people with similar interests to interact, look for help, gain knowledge from others, and foster a sense of community
