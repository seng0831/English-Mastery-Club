Model United Nations

Learn how to speak confidently!

Model UN, or MUN, is an educational simulation in which students take on the roles of delegates from various nations and participate in diplomatic discussions and debates. MUN gives participants the chance to hone their negotiation, public speaking, research and critical thinking skills by simulating the United Nations. It presents a special chance for students to investigate world issues, comprehend international relations, and cooperate to resolve issues from the real world.

Model United Nations to the Real United Nations

Our organization serves as a vital link bridging the Model United Nations (MUN) experience to the real United Nations (UN). We are aware of the immense value and potential that MUN simulations have in developing the world's future leaders. In light of this, we work to ensure that MUN participants have a smooth transition into the real UN and meaningful engagement there.
