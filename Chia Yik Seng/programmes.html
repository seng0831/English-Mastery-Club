Additional programmes we offer

English Language Test (IELTS)


IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is a widely recognized English language proficiency test designed to assess the language skills of individuals who want to study, work, or migrate to countries where English is the primary language of communication. It is accepted by over 10,000 organizations worldwide, including universities, employers, immigration authorities, and professional bodies.

Essential Public Speaking


Essential public speaking refers to the fundamental skills and techniques required to effectively communicate with an audience. It is an important skill set that can be applied in various settings, such as presentations, speeches, conferences, or even everyday conversations. Public speaking involves more than just delivering a message; it encompasses the art of engaging and influencing an audience, conveying information clearly, and delivering a compelling message.

Upcoming events


Model United Nations (MUN)

The Model United Nations (MUN) event at APU (Asia Pacific University) is a prestigious gathering where students from various educational institutions come together to simulate the workings of the United Nations. MUN conferences are typically organized to foster diplomacy, international relations, and public speaking skills among participants.

At the APU MUN event, participants will take on the roles of delegates representing different countries and engage in debates, negotiations, and resolution drafting on various global issues. The conference aims to provide a platform for students to explore complex global challenges, practice diplomacy, and develop a better understanding of international affairs.

Story-telling

The storytelling event at APU (Asia Pacific University) is a captivating gathering that celebrates the art of storytelling. It is a platform where individuals come together to share personal narratives, folktales, and creative tales that captivate and engage the audience.

The event aims to showcase the power of storytelling as a means of conveying emotions, experiences, and ideas. It encourages participants to harness their storytelling skills and creativity to craft compelling narratives that entertain, educate, and inspire.

During the event, participants will have the opportunity to take the stage and share their stories in front of a supportive audience.
